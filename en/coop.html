Chrysalide

Clic2000 is member of the cooperative Chrysalide created in Finistera in 2002.
The cooperative allows us to mutualize administrative ressources, legals, banking and accountancy. Each activity hosted in the cooperative represents a productive unit fully-fledged and autonomous. The entrepreneurs are salaried employees, the salary is produced by the sales revenue of their activity. The entrepreneur is the dirigeant of his activity, s·he is the one who develop it and the one who own its commercial identity.

Entrepreneurs from various professions are hosted : from services to individuals and entreprises to activities of production and trading, to professions of plastic arts and graphism, design, audiovisual, edition, live performances and construction industry.

We cooperate with other members of the cooperative and lead sometimes projects together.
