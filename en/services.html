Services

We develop your fully custom-made websites (static and dynamic websites) and host it, our favorites content management system are Bludit and PluXml.

We will support you in the inventory of fixtures of your existing infrastructure and the use of your digital tools. We make an audit of the existing system and we can then submit you concrete proposals, this is our consulting work.

We can provide all the digital tools you need for cooperation, we provide a large number of services :

  • You need your own collaborative system of files sharing ? Discover the application Nextcloud
  • You want an internal instant messaging (IM) system ? Give a try to Mattermost and its smart system of teams.
  • You need a smart management of your calendars and contacts list ? We provide the great project SOGo which integrate this functionalities with e-mail.
We train you in the use of these tools, we provide maintenance and support. These are not a source of concern but let you gain in trust and autonomy.

If you have special needs in terms of confidentiality, that your activity needs a lot of services, it is interesting to make you profit of your own dedicated server. We can then show you how to use Yunohost that will let you manage easily users accounts, their email addresses, the access to the services mentioned above.
