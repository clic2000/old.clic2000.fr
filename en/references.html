References

We have worked together:

Coups de pouce informatique

We have participated in the Vas-Y dispositive in the towns of Scrignac and St Thegonnec. During 8 workshops we have supported people over 60 years of age in the daily use of the computer.

Résidences Val d'Élorn

We cooperate on several projects: the implementation of a complete, high-performance and highly available email system, with the SOGo web client (email management, diaries and contacts), to the design of a dynamic website. We train and support members of the organization in the use and administration of these new tools.

Sébastien Bertholom

We build and host the website of Sébastien Bertholom, a piano technician. The website has been build with the classical HTML, CSS and JS and with Bulma framework. We host it on our infrastructure with our services of backups and metrology.

Maiwann.net

Maïtané Lenoir, an interface designer and user experience specialist, was looking for a file-sharing solution for her family and clients, she now trust us for hosting its Nextcloud server. She is autonomous in the creation of accounts and the addition of third-party apps, we take care of hosting and maintenance.

Rage.love

We host the rage.love server, an instance of the Florence decentralized social network, based on the protocol ActivityPub. Alternative to closed social networks like Facebook or Twitter whose general operation is inspired. Strong rules of moderation are applied to ensure an inclusive and enjoyable community.

Planning familial of Morlaix

We are helping the planning familial of Morlaix to get back their data and enforce digital confidentiality. To do so we provided them a dedicated server with the excellent project Yunohost with the applications SOGo for the emails and Nextcloud for sharing files.

Disc'n'Truck

We have set up the website of Disc'n'Truck, itinerant record store. The entrepreneur was looking for a reservation system for his records we have set it up with Wordpress and its famous plugin Woocommerce.

À La Carte Travel

We build the website of "À La Carte Travel" agency. We also supported the two entrepreneurs in the setting of a secured working environment (Passwords manager, full disk encryption, 2FA).
